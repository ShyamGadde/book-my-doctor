FAQ

To book a doctor's appointment online on Bookmydoctor, simply visit our website and go to the appointment booking section. Select your desired doctor, choose a suitable date and time slot, and provide your contact details. Once the appointment is successfully booked, you will receive a confirmation message.

When booking a doctor's appointment online, you will need to provide your name, contact number, preferred doctor, desired date and time for the appointment, and a brief description of your health concern. This information helps us to efficiently schedule your appointment with the appropriate doctor.

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel a doctor's appointment booked online through Bookmydoctor. Simply login to your account, go to the appointments section, and select the appointment you wish to reschedule or cancel. Follow the instructions provided to make the necessary changes. Please note that there may be a certain timeframe within which you can make changes to your appointment.
